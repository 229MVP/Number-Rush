**Number Rush** is a fast neon number puzzle. Place tiles into lanes, manage strikes, chain combos, and spend soft-currency power-ups to push your high score.

**Play now**
- **Classic Rush** — endless local runs with Multi, Swap, Bomb, Freeze, Shield, and Wild power-ups
- **Daily Tournament** — one seeded local challenge per UTC day, plus practice runs
- **Missions, Shop, Themes, Profile** — progress saved on your device

**Coming Soon**
