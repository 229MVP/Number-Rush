## Full description

**Number Rush** is a fast neon number puzzle. Place tiles into lanes, manage strikes, chain combos, and spend soft-currency power-ups to push your high score.

**Play now**
- **Classic Rush** — endless runs with Multi, Swap, Bomb, Freeze, Shield, and Wild power-ups
- **Daily Tournament** — seeded daily challenge (fair — no paid power-ups)
- **Ranked** — competitive runs without inventory advantages
- **Missions, Shop, Themes, Profile** — progression with optional cloud sync when signed in

**Optional monetization (when enabled in a release build)**
- Optional **rewarded ads** (revive / double Classic coins / daily free power-up)
- Limited **interstitial ads** after Classic runs only (not during play)
- **Remove Ads**, gem packs, and a one-time **Starter Bundle** via App Store / Google Play
- **Number Rush Club** subscription architecture exists but is **not launched** until checklist complete

**Not included**
- No cash prizes, withdrawals, or betting
- No purchasable Ranked advantages or Daily attempt buys

**Beta / build notes**
- Development uses **test ads** and **sandbox purchases** until production IDs are confirmed
- Sound effects and music may be **placeholder** quality in early builds

Place. Stack. Hit the target.
