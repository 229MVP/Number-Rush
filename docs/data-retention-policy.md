# Data Retention Policy (Draft)

| Category | Suggested retention | Notes |
|----------|---------------------|-------|
| Accepted competitive results | Indefinite while product active | Needed for leaderboards / disputes |
| Rejected / pending submissions | 90 days | Fraud review window |
| Raw run event payloads | 30–90 days | Minimize PII |
| Operator audit logs | 2+ years | Do not allow operator deletion |
| Player reports | 1 year after close | Hide reporter identity |
| Purchase / ad reward transactions | Financial retention (multi-year) | Legal / store requirements |
| Deleted-account stubs | As required for fraud/finance | Follow privacy policy |

Scheduled cleanup functions must not run until this policy is approved.
