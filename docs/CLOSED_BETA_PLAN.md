# Closed Beta Plan

## Cohort 1 — 10 trusted testers (3–5 days)
Core Classic navigation, Daily practice, no production billing.

## Cohort 2 — 25–50 testers (7 days)
Progression, sync, Ranked tickets (sandbox), feedback submissions.

## Cohort 3 — 100–250 testers (7–14 days)
Events, announcements, monetization sandbox, load observation.

Entry/exit: crash rate, sync errors, validation rejects, support volume.  
Do not enable production billing until sandbox fulfillment is verified.
